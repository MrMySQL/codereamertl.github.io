Career Counsellor

Career Counsellor is a special advisor on all matters of career development who helps all our new employees adapt and grow from their first working days. Career Counsellor is responsible for employees’ professional development and provides necessary information to help our colleagues achieve a career growth.

Analyzes

Your professional experience; personal skills; personal wishes and views on your career.

Knows standard requirements to positions

Career Counsellor knows our company’s standard requirements to every position and can advise our colleagues on which skills to develop in order to get promotion or a desired position in our company.

Draws professional and career opportunities for you

Matches your skills with company needs; encourages the development of your potential; suggest the most appropriate way to gain new knowledge.

Advises you what opportunities are good for you

Helps you to see better possibilities of applying your talents and skills.

Professional Growth

Each of us is interested in opportunities for professional growth. This is an integral part of planning your future. Talent Management system was developed to help each and every of our colleagues to define which steps to take to get closer to reaching their aims.

 

Training/Presentation

Employee creates and gives training/presentation to his/her colleagues in which his/her presentation skills are evaluated.

Performance Appraisal

Employee receives objective feedback on his/her current work. Based on this feedback the recommendations are being given that will help improve employee's skills and competences to achieve higher performance.

Knowledge Evaluation

Knowledge evaluation defines for each of our colleagues the opportunities of further development and improvement of technical knowledge and also further learning process is planned.

English Level Test

It is conducted by Language School in order to evaluate the level of English knowledge of our employees.

IT Experience

Appropriate experience in IT is required for promotion.

External Certification

Certification is the way to confirm the level of professionalism of our colleagues, the presence of modern, necessary for the market set of skills which are highly evaluated by employer and a client.



Continuous and gradual professional and personal growth is sure to bring promotion. Each completed stage is a step to a desirable position. It’s important to choose your career path correctly, in which you will be helped by our Career Counsellors and a specially developed Career Map.
